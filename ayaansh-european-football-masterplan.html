FIFA Regulations & Visa Pathways

Critical legal framework for international transfer of minors under 18.

FIFA Article 19 — The Rule

FIFA prohibits international transfer of players under 18 years old. This is an absolute rule with only 5 narrow exceptions. Without an approved exception, no club can register Ayaansh.

Exception #1: Parental Relocation (YOUR PATH)

If parents move to the club's country for non-football reasons (e.g., work, career), the child can register with a local club. This is the most commonly approved exception.

Requirements:

1. Both parents (or primary guardian) must relocate
2. Employment contracts proving career-driven move (NOT football-driven)
3. Housing proof in new country
4. School enrollment for the player
5. Health insurance documentation
6. Application to FIFA Players' Status Committee
7. International Transfer Certificate (ITC) from AIFF

Critical: Spain's May 2025 Visa Change

Spain removed eligibility for student visas for sports academies in May 2025. This means you cannot get Ayaansh a student visa to attend a football academy. The ONLY viable route is parent employment-based relocation, which then triggers the FIFA Article 19 exception naturally.

Scam Protection Guide

Critical awareness for Indian families seeking European football opportunities. Scammers heavily target Indian families.

Red Flags — AVOID

Upfront payment requests

Legitimate clubs NEVER charge for trials. FIFA rules state inviting clubs must cover travel, accommodation, and meals for trial players.

Contact via Instagram DM, WhatsApp, or personal Gmail

Real clubs only contact through official email (@villarrealcf.es, @sevillafc.es). Any agent using @gmail.com or social media DMs is suspect.

"Guaranteed professional contract" promises

No one can guarantee a contract before trials. This is the #1 manipulation tactic used against Indian families.

Pressure to decide quickly or pay via Western Union/crypto

Legitimate processes take weeks/months. Urgency is a manipulation tool.

Scouts "discovering" your child on social media

Real scouts attend recognized tournaments and work through official channels, not Instagram.

Green Flags — TRUST

Official club email domains

@valenciacf.es, @villarrealcf.es, @sevillafc.es, @acmilan.com, @inter.it, @atalanta.it

FIFA-licensed agents

Verify at: inside.fifa.com/transfer-system/agents — this is the ONLY official directory.

Club covers trial costs

Legitimate clubs that want to assess a player will cover travel and accommodation costs.

Established organizations with verifiable track records

FCV Football Academy, Spanish Soccer Trials, IDA Rome — all have verifiable histories and transparent processes.
